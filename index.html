Agnij

Portable Free Energy

Agnij is a thermoelectric power generator which can be placed around any hot vessel or stove to utilise waste heat and generate electricity sufficient enough to charge mobile phones and LED lights. This is affordable and portable solution providing energy on demand to users like rural women, people in relief camps, trekkers/hikers, etc.

WHR

Enabler for Industries

Waste heat recovery (WHR) system for industries is a scaled version of Agnij, technologically optimised and designed especially for high-efficiency and rugged scenarios. This system is easily integrable with existing industrial equipments, providing consistent inhouse power generation in addition to advantages of going ‘Green’.

WEE accelerator for women entrepreneurship and empowerment

October, 2018

WEE Mentorship Program at IIT Delhi is a path breaking program where a batch of 30 women are trained in entrepreneurship every six months. Step by step guidance is provided to strengthen and validate the business idea. Women entrepreneurs are connected to potential investors and buyers for their products so that they can take their business idea to a financially sustainable enterprise.
